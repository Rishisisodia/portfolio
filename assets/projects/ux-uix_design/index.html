Redesign of Myntra

About Myntra

Myntra, a prominent online fashion destination in India, serves as a vast virtual marketplace for clothing, shoes, and various fashion items. With a considerable presence over the years, it has become the go-to platform for numerous individuals seeking to purchase items of their preference. Within this exploration, we delve into enhancing the Myntra experience, aiming to streamline navigation and elevate user satisfaction. Our focus spans three pivotal aspects of the application: the primary screen that greets users upon opening, the pages dedicated to browsing and selecting desired items, and the seamless payment process. Our objective is to transform Myntra into an exceptional shopping destination, where users effortlessly discover their preferred items, fostering a delightful and efficient shopping journey. Join us in uncovering ways to elevate Myntra to new heights and provide users with an even more remarkable online shopping experience!

Research Strategy - Myntra User Experience

I may not have followed the formal research protocols entirely, but I undertook a constructive initiative. Rather than delving into official procedures, I perused the sentiments shared by users about Myntra in the app store reviews. In the process, I identified aspects that irk me personally while navigating the app.

Compiling these prevalent grievances and challenges, I embarked on a quest to enhance the user experience of Myntra. My primary emphasis lies in refining the app's visual aesthetics and functionality, aligning with the apparent desires of the user base.

Personas

Name: Olivia

Age: 25

Shopping Style: Efficient and Organized

Olivia is a busy professional who values her time and seeks efficiency in her online shopping experience.
